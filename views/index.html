Gonioscopy Lens Eye images

About

Web Application for Eye care where Doctor will see the all the patient details and their respective eye images taken from the Gonioscopy android application, which is stored in centralised database. The patient eye images will be captured using Gonioscopy Android application, in which Gonio lens device in attached to the Mobile device.The captured eye images will be stored in centralised database along with Patient ID and image ID. The eye images will be captured in Android application in burst mode and stored in centralised database. Eye images took from the centralised database will be photo stacked before it will be displayed to doctor.Photo Stacking a process of combining the two or more photos into single photos.
